# Foundations&#x20;

In today’s digital economy, the intersection of Artificial Intelligence (AI) automation and Computer Vision (CV) is reshaping industries from self-driving cars and automated manufacturing lines to smart retail and intelligent workflow automation.

This foundational course offers a comprehensive introduction to the core principles, technologies, and practical applications of AI-driven automation and visual data processing. Designed for beginners and transitioning professionals, this course bridges the gap between theoretical AI concepts and hands-on, real-world deployment. You will learn how to train machines to "see," interpret visual data, and build intelligent workflows that automate repetitive tasks, supercharging efficiency without requiring a PhD in mathematics. (But we will learn the math that is needed anyways.)
